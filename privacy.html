Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026 • Private pilot

What stays on your iPhone

Imported audio files and full lyric transcriptions remain in CleanCue’s protected app storage on your iPhone. CleanCue does not upload them.

What the pilot service stores

If you connect pilot sync, CleanCue stores your email address, events, emcee notes, compact track-analysis summaries, guest song requests, feedback, and limited product-use events. Track summaries may include title, artist, BPM, key, confidence, and explicit-category counts—but not audio or full transcripts.

Guest requests

Guests may provide a song, artist, name, and message. The service creates a short-lived one-way hash from network and browser information only for spam rate limiting; it does not retain the raw IP address in the application database.

Tracking and sale

CleanCue contains no ads and does not track you across apps or websites. We do not sell personal information.

Deletion and contact

DJs can delete local and synced account data inside Pilot Settings. Guests or pilots may request help through the support page.

Accuracy

Automated lyric, BPM, and key analysis may be wrong. Users must review results before relying on them at an event.
