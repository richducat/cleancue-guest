CleanCue Support

CleanCue is currently a private pilot. Include whether you use the iPhone app or Android web pilot, your device/browser version, and the exact step that failed.

Common fixes

  • Android audio analysis: try MP3 or M4A, keep the page open while BPM/key are estimated, and paste a reviewed timed transcript for lyric flags.
  • No lyric flags: confirm timestamps use [mm:ss] and treat every automated result as review-required.
  • Device guest preview: open the link in the same browser as the DJ workspace. Cross-device requests are not active and require both the pilot backend and Android DJ sync integration.
  • Privacy: Android users choose Library → Delete my browser data; iPhone users use Pilot Settings → Delete account data.
