CleanCue Support

CleanCue is currently a private pilot. Include your app version, iPhone model, and the exact step that failed when asking for help.

Common fixes

  • Audio analysis: confirm the file is MP3, M4A, or WAV and grant Speech Recognition permission.
  • No lyric flags: treat the result as unverified when vocals are heavily processed or buried in the mix.
  • Guest link: confirm the event is live and requests are enabled.
  • Privacy: delete all local and synced data from Pilot Settings → Delete account data.
